Welcome!

The 22 Project is dedicated to helping improve the lives and well-being of as many veterans as possible. Part of our goal is ensuring you are well-informed on what you can expect while you're here in South Florida. Below you'll find some important information to reference while you prepare for and begin your treatment.

Before Arrival

  • Receive flight itinerary (Contact us ASAP if you have not received this 14 days prior to start date)
  • Complete intake paperwork for HBOT office
  • Things to note about HBOT treatment:
    • Cotton clothing ONLY
    • Nothing else allowed in chamber (books, electronics, etc.) - due to pure oxygen safety concerns
    • Common side effects: fatigue, headaches, ear ringing
    • Maintain a healthy diet - no drugs/alcohol, certain medications (**), daily exercise (Recommended to get the most out of your treatment)
